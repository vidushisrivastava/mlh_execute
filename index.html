ABOUT US

At E-Cell we attempt to foster new values amongst young individuals to make them an exemplary engineer imbibed with the idea of productive, innovative and independent thinking.
Major League Hacking (MLH) is the official student hackathon league. Every year, they power innumerous competitions that inspire innovation, cultivate communities and teach computer science skills to more than 65,000 students around the world. MLH is an engaged and passionate maker community, consisting of the next generation of technology leaders and entrepreneurs.

Execute will provide the essential platform for those who strive to create a change in the society through their out of the box ideas, zeal and ardour. Execute will sprint your mind, make you brainstorm extensively and stretch you beyond your limits. It is one of a kind experience for amateurs, professionals and most importantly hustlers. Be a part of Execute and show your worth and potential to the world of transforming dreams into reality.

TIMELINE


  • 19 DEC'20
  • 20 DEC'20

+
INAUGURATION

The Hackathon will be inaugurated by the esteemed dignitaries, after which an overview and guidelines of the event will be announced. The ‘execution’ of transforming ordinary into extraordinary will commence.

+
READY.SET.HACK.

The problem statement will be released and the hackers will be starting their vigorous brainstorm session. Everyone will be having 24 hours to mould and shape the solution to the problem.

+
WORKSHOPS

At night there would be fun activities and game nights for the hustlers to pause and recharge themselves. Informative workshops would be conducted to hone and polish the skillset of the hackers.

+
SUBMISSION

The ticking of the clock would be over, and the final solution would be submitted. The esteemed judges would be evaluating and scoring each solution on the basis of innovation, structuring, creativity and the way it has been executed.

+
THE ENDGAME

The competition will reach its final stage - the endgame when the ideas would further be shortlisted. The top solutions would then be going to the final round.

+
PRESENTATION

The top ideas who reached the finale would have to present their idea in front of the judges. The presentation would test your idea, your approach and how smoothly you sell the solution.

FAQS

How to register for the Hackathon?

All you have to do is to enter your e-mail address and fill out the necessary details and you are ready to go. Yes! The hackathon is free of cost!

How many members are required to form a team?

You can either participate individually or in a group. ( maximum people in 1 team = 5)

Who can participate?

This hackathon is for those who wish to learn and grow. Be it a tech-enthusiasts, a coding professional or amateurs who want to learn. The stage is open for all.

Why should I participate?

Hackathons are a great way to expand your network, learn from the best and also brainstorm and unleash your potential to bring out the best in you! Also the winners would be getting some amazing prizes and hampers.

Are there any prerequisites?
